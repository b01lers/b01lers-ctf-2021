    C- Ratings look-up

    After every trip, drivers and riders are able to rate each other on a scale from 1 to 5. An average of those ratings is associated with a users account and is displayed to other users for whom they provide or receive services. For example, rider ratings are available to drivers from whom they request transportation, and driver ratings are available to their riders.
